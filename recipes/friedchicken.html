Triple-Dipped Fried Chicken

Description

This fried chicken is no ordinary fried chicken. This type of fried chicken yields the crispiest, spiciest, homemade fried chicken ever tasted. It is equally good served hot or cold and it is good in any type of picnic or a get together.

Ingredients

  • 1 quart vegetable oil for frying
  • 4.3 cups all-purpose flour, divided
  • 1.5 tablespoons garlic salt
  • 1 tablespoon ground black pepper
  • 1 tablespoon paprika
  • 0.5 teaspoon poultry seasoning
  • 1.5 cups beer
  • 2 egg yolks, beaten
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 0.25 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1(3 pound) whole chicken, cut into pieces
